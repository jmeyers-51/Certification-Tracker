New York State Security Guard Training Requirements — NYS GBL §89-n & NYS DOS
8-Hour Pre-Assignment
Required once before applying for a NYS Security Guard Registration Card. Must be completed at a DCJS-approved school. NY DCJS →
16-Hour On-the-Job Training (OJT)
Must be completed within 90 days of initial employment. One-time requirement specific to the employer's site. NYS DOS →
8-Hour Annual In-Service
Required every calendar year the guard holds a registration. Two completions required per 2-year license cycle for renewal eligibility. NYS DOS →
License Renewal (2-Year Cycle)
NYS Security Guard Registration Cards are valid for two years. Renewal available ~90 days before expiry via NYS Division of Licensing Services. NYS DOS →
